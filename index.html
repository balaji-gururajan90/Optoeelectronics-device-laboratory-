Who We Are

Our Focus

Advancing the frontiers of optoelectronic devices through innovative materials, fabrication techniques, and computational modeling.

☀️

Thin-Film & Transparent Solar Cells

Developing novel materials and device architectures for photovoltaic applications, focusing on transparent and semi-transparent solar cells with enhanced efficiency and stability.

💎

Wide Band Gap Semiconductors

Investigating oxide semiconductors and wide band gap materials for UV and X-ray photodetectors with applications in sensing and imaging.

🔬

2D Materials

Exploring transition metal dichalcogenides and heterostructures for next-generation optoelectronic devices with enhanced quantum efficiency.

💻

Computation

Utilizing COMSOL and SCAPS simulation tools to optimize device architectures and predict performance characteristics.
